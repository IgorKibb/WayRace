Try it out now and see how easy it is to keep track of your time and record your laps with our online stopwatch app.

Get the Most Out of Our Online Stopwatch App: A Step-by-Step Guide

  • Start the stopwatch:

    To start timing, simply click the “Start” button. The stopwatch will immediately begin counting.

  • Pause the stopwatch:

    If you need to pause the stopwatch, just click the “Stop” button. The time will be paused and you can resume it at any time by clicking “Start” again.

  • Record laps:

    To record a lap, click the “Lap” button while the stopwatch is running. This will create a thread with the recorded time up to that point. You can record as many laps as you want.

  • Reset the stopwatch:

    If you want to erase the data and start timing again, click the “Reset” button. This will erase all recorded information and allow you to start timing again.
